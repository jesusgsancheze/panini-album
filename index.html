Dropbox Sync

Sync your collection to your own Dropbox. Your album file lives in Apps/<app name>/album.json in your Dropbox — only you can see it.

To sync your collection across devices, connect a Dropbox app:

  1. Go to dropbox.com/developers/apps → Create app.
  2. Choose Scoped access, then App folder (safest — only sees its own folder).
  3. Name it anything (e.g. panini-album).
  4. In the Permissions tab, check files.content.write and files.content.read, then Submit.
  5. In Settings, copy the App key.

  7. Paste the App key below and click Connect.
